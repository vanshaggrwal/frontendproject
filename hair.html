How to prevent hairfall?

Hair loss is most commonly caused by your genetics and becomes more common in men and women with age. The second most common cause is telogen effluvium. This condition occurs when more of your hair enters the rest cycle, during which it falls out.


How to Prevent faded hair colour?

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf,or using hair sunscreen.


How to prevent frizzy hair??

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type and texture. This will help to hydrate and nourish your hair,which will reduce fizz.


How to prevent dry And irritated scalp?

Use a scalp moisturing treatment or oil to hydrate and nourisg your scalp, which will help to reduce dryness and irritation.
